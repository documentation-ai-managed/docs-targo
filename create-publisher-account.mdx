---
title: Create Publisher Account
---
1. **Register** on <a href="https://targo.io" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https\://targo.io</a> and create your Publisher account.

   - Please **sign up using a business email** or the **developer email associated with your app or store listing**. This helps us verify ownership and speed up the approval process.

2. **Verify your email** to activate your account.

3. **Wait for account approval.** Our team reviews each application manually to ensure traffic quality and platform compatibility.

   **Approval is based on:**

   - App / website legitimacy and functionality

   - User traffic quality

   - Developer identity or store presence

   - Ratings & reviews if your app is published in an app store

   - **Overall concept and purpose** of the app or website (it must follow legal and ethical guidelines)

<Callout kind="alert" collapsed="false">
  The approval process is handled manually and usually takes up to 24 hours, depending on review volume **(excluding Saturdays and Sundays).** **If we require additional information to verify your application, our team will reach out to you directly.**
</Callout>
