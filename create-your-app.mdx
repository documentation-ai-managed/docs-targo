---
title: Create Your App
---
1. Navigate to the **Apps** section from the sidebar.

2. Click **+ Add New App**.

3. Fill in all required details and click **Submit**.

4. After creation, you may configure:

   - **Currency Multiplier** (how many coins per $1)

   - **Currency Label** (e.g., Coins, Points, Gems)

   - **Tabs / Layout settings**

   - **Your Postback URL** Then click **Save** to apply changes.

<Callout kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Your **first app is automatically approved**, based on the app information you provided during signup. You can add **up to 10 apps** in total. Each additional app **must go through manual review** before going live.
</Callout>
